Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

The creation of something new or the improvement of existing things. It involves introducing new ideas, methods, and products to solve problems or create value.

Sustainability

Meeting the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs.It focuses on balancing economic, 2 social, and environmental factors.

Infrastructure

The basic physical systems of a country or region, such as roads, bridges, power supplies, and buildings. It is essential for economic development and social well-being.

Empowerment

The process of increasing the autonomy and self-determination of individuals and communities. It involves providing people with the resources, skills, and confidence to take control of their own lives and make decisions that affect them.

Bangladesh's first Satellite

Date: 11/01/2025

Time: 08.05 PM

Launched in 2018, Bangladesh's first geostationary communication satellite. It provides high-speed internet, television broadcasting, disaster management, and distance learning services across the country. This satellite has significantly improved communication infrastructure and connectivity in Bangladesh.

Role of Satellites in Bangladesh

Date: 11/01/2025

Time: 08.25 PM

Satellites play a crucial role in various sectors of Bangladesh. They are used for weather forecasting, disaster management, telecommunications, remote sensing, and navigation. Satellites enable efficient and timely responses to natural disasters like floods and cyclones. They also support the development of precision agriculture, resource management, and environmental monitoring.

Future of Satellite Technology in Bangladesh

Date: 11/01/2025

Time: 09.20 PM

Bangladesh is actively exploring future satellite missions. Plans include launching more satellites for advanced communication services, high-resolution earth observation, and space research. These endeavors aim to further enhance the country's technological capabilities, contribute to economic growth, and improve the quality of life for its citizens.
